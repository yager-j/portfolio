Live Concert Website Design

Design for a live concert website for multiple screen sizes, completed in a team with two of my classmates as part of my studies.

UX//UI Design – Figma – Phone – Tablet – Desktop

The website allows users to search for and filter concerts, and to buy tickets to stream a concert or attend live. Users stream concerts directly in the website.

Users can write concert reviews and read reviews written by other users. Artist and venue pages show upcoming and previous concerts by artist and venue. Users create a profile and can follow other users, artists and venues.
