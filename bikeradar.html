Bike Radar

An app that uses the CityBikes API to provide realtime information about bike sharing networks all over the world.

SwiftUI – Async/Await – MapKit – CityBikes API

After selecting a city, the user can see all bike sharing stations in the city on a map; tapping on a station gives the user real-time information about the number of free bikes and empty slots as well as the distance, travel time and route to the station from the user's current location.

The look around feature can be used to visually explore the area around the bike sharing station, this is useful if the user is unfamiliar with an area of the city or if the station is difficult to find.

In the next iteration of the app, I plan to build an interactive home screen widget that shows bike sharing station locations near the user as well as easily accessible information about the number of free bikes and available slots at the user's favourite stations, providing realtime information at a glance and helping users to quickly and easily plan their trip.
