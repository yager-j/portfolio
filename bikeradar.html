Bike Radar

An app that uses the CityBikes API to give the user realtime information about bike sharing networks all over the world.

SwiftUI – Async/Await – MapKit

The map shows all bike sharing stations in the network and city selected by the user, with information about the number of free bikes and empty slots at each station. The app shows the distance and travel time from the user's current location and the user can choose to show the route to the station from their current location.

The user can use the look around feature to explore the area around the bike sharing station. This might be helpful e.g. in the case that the station is difficult to find.

In the next iteration, I plan to add an interactive home screen widget that shows bike sharing station locations near the user and shows easily accessible information about the number of free bikes and available slots at the user's favorite stations, giving the user information at a glance and helping them to plan their trip on the fly.
